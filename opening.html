About the author

Opening Hours

General Opening Hours

SFM retailers are open from 7am every day except Christmas Day.

Our retailers generally close at 4pm Monday - Thursday, however for your convenience some of our retailers are open until 5pm Friday - Sunday. Fisherman's Wharf Restaurant dinner service is open until 11pm every night. For more detailed information on your favourite retailer, including trading hours and contact numbers, click here>

Office & Administration Hours

SFM office hours are from 7am to 4.30pm weekdays. We are in the office every week day, except public holidays.

Auction Times

SFM's wholesale auction commences at 5:30 am every week day apart from public holidays. Please note, public access to the wholesale auction is via Behind the Scenes Tours only, see below for details.

Tours

SFM Behind the Scenes Tours are held every weekday at 6.40 am - bookings are required to avoid disappointment. Click here for further information on tours> or phone +61 9004 1108
